1. Get a continuous glucose monitor

We use the Abbott Freestyle Libre 14 day sensor, which costs $40 for 2 weeks.


In the US, you need a prescription from your doctor to get one.


Elsewhere, you can buy them over the counter at a pharmacy or online.
